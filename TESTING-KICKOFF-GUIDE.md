## ⚠️ Potential Issues & Solutions

### **Issue 1: Team members don't have time**
**Solution:**
- Reduce scope
- Extend timeline
- Prioritize critical test cases only

### **Issue 2: Too many bugs found**
**Solution:**
- Focus on critical bugs first
- Fix and retest incrementally
- Extend timeline if needed

### **Issue 3: Team members don't understand GST software**
**Solution:**
- Provide demo video
- Walk through the application
- Pair testing sessions

### **Issue 4: Test environment not accessible**
**Solution:**
- Deploy to cloud (Vercel/Railway)
- Provide VPN access
- Share screen via Zoom

### **Issue 5: Low participation**
**Solution:**
- Daily reminders
- Motivate with recognition
- Make it fun (gamify)
